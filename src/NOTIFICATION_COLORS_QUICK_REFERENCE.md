## **COLOR PSYCHOLOGY**

### **Why These Colors Work:**

**💜 PURPLE (Connection)**
- Represents luxury, specialness, uniqueness
- Already used for "Someone Special" in the app
- Creates emotional warmth and connection
- "Someone cares about me enough to send this"

**🔵 BLUE (Personal)**
- Trust, calm, introspection, self-reflection
- Personal journey color
- Represents the "you talking to yourself" aspect
- "This is MY story, MY memories"

**💚 GREEN (Interaction)**
- Active, growing, engaged, alive
- Two-way conversation color
- Represents ongoing dialogue
- "We're connecting, conversing, engaging"

**🟡 GOLD (Celebration)**
- Success, reward, achievement, winning
- Universal celebration color
- Creates pride and motivation
- "I did it! I accomplished something!"

**🔴 RED (Urgent)**
- Alert, warning, attention, action
- Universal "stop and look" signal
- Creates urgency without panic
- "Hey, I need you to fix this"

**⚪ SLATE (System)**
- Neutral, calm, informational
- Background information color
- Non-intrusive and subtle
- "Just letting you know"
